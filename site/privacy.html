Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

Overview

TecVooDoo LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates tecvoodoo.com and related games and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Google, Facebook, or Magic Link email, we receive:

  • Your email address
  • Display name (if provided by the authentication provider)

We do not receive or store your password. Authentication is handled securely by third-party providers (Google, Facebook) or passwordless magic links.

Game Data

When you play our games, we store:

  • Game session data (moves, scores, game state)
  • Your chosen display name and color preferences
  • Game history for "My Active Games" feature

Anonymous/Guest Users

If you play as a guest without signing in, we create an anonymous session. This data is temporary and not linked to any personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide multiplayer game functionality
  • To save and sync your games across devices
  • To display your name to opponents in online games
  • To improve our games and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With other players in multiplayer games (display name only)
  • With service providers who help us operate our services (Supabase for database, Cloudflare for hosting)
  • If required by law

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any future communications

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please email privacy@tecvoodoo.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your display name and color preferences
  • Maintain your authentication session
  • Track local game history for guests

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@tecvoodoo.com
  • Website: tecvoodoo.com/contact
